The Pre Fab Academy Workshop:

The pre-fab academy workshop is a tailor made 30-day experimental course organised by Kerala Startup Mission and Fab Academy to prepare participants for the 6 month Fab Academy course which spans from January 2016 to July 2016. The two participating Fablabs are FabLab Trivandrum and FabLab Kochi.

What am I trying to do here?

I head the activities at FabLab Kochi here in Startup Village and I am taking this pilot program to make myself ready for 6 months Fab Academy course which starts on January 2016. Fab Academy course will also equip me with skills and knowledge with which I can manage operations at Kochi FabLab. It will also help me train others and share my knowledge with new asirants.

The plan

The two awesome Fab Trainers Luciano Betoldi and Francisco will be conducting training at both FabLabs.

I will surely miss the second week of training as my wife is due for delivery, I am planning on catching up with all the stuff taught in the second week in the remaining weeks. However, I will be updating my documentation page in chronological order with the stuff that I missed and the stuff that I learned after that.
